Hyper text markup language

HTML stands for HyperText Markup Language. It is the standard language used to create and design web pages. HTML structures the content on the web and tells the browser how to display text, images, links, and other elements on a webpage.

What are HTML Headings?

Headings in HTML are used to define the structure of content on a webpage. They are usually the titles or subtitles of different sections having different fontt szizes. It ranges from h1 to h6 where h1 is the most important and h6 is the least important or smallest.
